This article examines the common organizational problem where design system documentation exists across multiple platforms and tools but lacks clear ownership, making it difficult to keep consistent and up to date. The post explores how fragmented documentation ownership leads to gaps in quality and adoption, and proposes strategies for teams to establish accountability and centralize their design system docs.
